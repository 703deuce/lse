Listing Problems Are Costing You Customers

Your business information may already be spread across dozens of platforms—and not all of it is accurate.

Inconsistent NAP

Different versions of your name, address and phone number across platforms confuse customers and search engines.

Duplicate Listings

Multiple profiles for the same business split reviews, create confusion and make management harder.

Wrong Phone Numbers

Old or disconnected numbers send ready-to-buy customers straight to a competitor or voicemail.

Outdated Addresses

Previous locations and moved offices can linger online for years, sending customers to the wrong place.

What Are Local Citations?

A local citation is an online mention of your business information—usually your name, address and phone number (NAP). Complete listings may also include your website, categories, hours, photos, services and social profiles.

Your Business Information Is Already Spread Across the Web

Your company may appear on search engines, map platforms, business directories, review websites, social profiles, industry directories, data providers and mobile apps. Once incorrect information begins spreading, customers struggle to contact you and search engines receive conflicting signals.

Citation Building Is Not Just Submitting Your Business Everywhere

Many services upload your information to the largest list of directories they can find. We focus on relevance and accuracy based on your country, city, industry, business type, existing listings and competitors.

Data Aggregator and Distribution Review

We evaluate whether aggregator-style distribution is appropriate and make it clear when a listing is directly created, submitted through a provider, pending distribution or dependent on a third-party platform.

Login Credentials and Listing Ownership

Where platforms permit direct account ownership, we document available access information including listing URL, submission status, account email and verification status. You should not be permanently locked out of your own listings.

Citation Building for New Businesses

New businesses benefit from establishing consistent information from the beginning—core platform setup, primary local directories, industry profiles, accurate NAP formatting and category selection before large-scale submissions begin.

Citation Cleanup for Established Businesses

Established businesses may need old addresses, former phone numbers, incorrect URLs, duplicate listings, rebranding fixes and inaccurate categories addressed before new submissions are created.

Multi-Location Citation Building

Each location needs unique address, phone number, landing page, hours, categories and separate verification. We organize location data before submissions begin and track each office separately.

What Citations Cannot Do by Themselves

Citations cannot replace a weak website, missing service pages, poor reviews, low authority or an incomplete Google Business Profile. They are part of the foundation—not the entire building. Pair them with a complete local SEO strategy including Google Maps SEO services.

Manual Citation Building Versus Automated Distribution

Manual work allows more control over categories, descriptions, duplicate checking and ownership. Automated distribution can submit faster but may require recurring payments or provide less profile control.
