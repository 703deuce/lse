Meet Anthony Johnson

Owner of Local SEO Express

I have been working in digital marketing for 17 years. During that time, I have watched search engines, websites, advertising platforms and customer behavior change repeatedly. But one thing has remained the same: a business cannot win customers online if people cannot find it.

That problem is especially important for local businesses. A plumber may provide better service than every company around them. A dentist may have more experience. A contractor may produce higher-quality work. But when those businesses are missing from Google Maps and local search, customers often call the company they see first.

I created Local SEO Express to help businesses close that visibility gap—not with a generic checklist, vague promises, or rankings that have no connection to revenue. The focus is on building a stronger local search presence that produces more calls, quote requests, appointments and customers.

Local SEO Should Produce More Than Rankings

A business can rank for dozens of keywords and still struggle to generate leads.

That happens when the campaign focuses on activity instead of outcomes—publishing articles nobody searches for, building irrelevant links, creating city pages with no value, tracking rankings without tracking phone calls, or optimizing a Google Business Profile while ignoring the website.

The work may look busy. But busy does not mean effective. We believe local SEO should be connected to the way your business grows.

We Start With the Market

There is plenty of general SEO advice online. But the businesses currently ranking in your market show us what Google is actually rewarding.

We examine Google Maps results, organic rankings, competitor websites, profiles, service pages, reviews, citations, backlinks, content and authority—then compare those companies with your business to determine what matters most and what should happen first.

What We Will Not Promise

  • We will not guarantee a number-one ranking—Google controls the search results and rankings change based on location, proximity, competition, reviews, website strength and algorithm updates.
  • We will not promise hundreds of meaningless backlinks or citations simply to make a package look larger.
  • We will not pretend every business needs the most expensive plan.
  • What we can do is identify the problems holding your business back, build a strategy around realistic opportunities and track whether visibility and leads are improving.
