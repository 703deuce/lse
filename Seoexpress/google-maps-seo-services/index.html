How Google Maps Rankings Work

It's Not Just Your Google Business Profile

Google considers three main factors to decide which businesses show up in the Map Pack.

Relevance

How closely does the business match what the customer searched for?

  • GBP categories
  • Services
  • Website content
  • Keywords

Distance

How close is the business to the person searching or the location in the search?

  • Searcher's location
  • Business address
  • Service area
  • Proximity

Prominence

How established and trusted does the business appear?

  • Reviews
  • Citations
  • Website authority
  • Backlinks

We optimize the factors you can control and build the signals that help your business outrank your competitors.
